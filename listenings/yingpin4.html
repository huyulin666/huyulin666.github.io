Leadership is a word we hear a lot nowadays. Every time we turn on the news, we hear about how important leadership is. Especially strong leadership.
领导力是我们现在经常听到的一个词。每次我们打开新闻,我们都会听说领导力是多么重要。特别是强有力的领导。

American presidents often talk about the importance of leadership. I suppose they’re right. It’s important that someone takes responsibility and shows leadership.
美国总统经常谈论领导力的重要性。我想他们是对的。重要的是要有人负起责任并表现出领导力。

With the right leaders, we can achieve success. But not every leader shows leadership. In many countries around the world, there are corrupt leaders. They do not have good leadership skills; they simply control people by fear.
有了正确的领导者，我们就能取得成功。但并非所有领导人都表现出领导力。世界上许多国家都有腐败的领导人。他们没有很好的领导能力；他们只是通过恐惧来控制人们。

Good leadership is being respected by everyone and getting the job done. It’s important to have good leadership in companies too, except many of the banks’ leaders have not shown such good leadership.
良好的领导能力是每个人都尊重并完成工作。在公司中拥有良好的领导能力也很重要，除非许多银行的领导没有表现出如此良好的领导能力。
