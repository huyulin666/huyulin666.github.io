October 13 ,Mark is practicing badminton.
10月13日,马克在练羽毛球。
He hit the birdie 27 times, Then it landed on the floor.
他连击羽球二十七次后球落在地上。
Megan doesn't play badminton very often now, But she still has her racket.
梅根现在不常打羽毛球了，但是她还留着她的球拍。
Mark plays badminton with Jim, They can play Rob and Megan!They can set up a net in the park.
马克和吉姆一起打羽毛球，他们可以和罗柏、梅根对打！他们可以在公园里架个网。
Who will win? And who will lose?
谁会赢？ 谁会输呢？

Mark, what are you doing?
马克，你在做什么？
Just a minute . . . 25, 26, 27.
等一下…25、26、27。
Oh, I missed!
哦，我漏接了！
Yes, you did. The birdie landed on the floor.
对啊，你漏接了，羽毛球掉在地上。
But I hit it 27 times first!
不过在那之前，我打了二十七次喔！
Why are you practicing badminton?
你为什么要练习羽毛球？
Because I love badminton!
因为我很爱打羽毛球啊！
It's my new favorite sport.
这是我最近最爱的运动。
Badminton is not easy to play.
羽毛球不好打。
I know. So I am practicing.
我知道啊， 所以我要练习。

I played badminton a lot in high school.
我高中时常打羽毛球，
I was pretty good.
那时我蛮厉害的。
Do you still play?
你现在还打吗？
A little. I don't play very often now.
偶尔。我现在不太常打了，
But I still have my badminton racket!
但是我还保留着我的羽球拍！
I like to play badminton, too.
我也喜欢打羽毛球，
I play it at the park sometimes.
有时会在公园里打。
I play badminton with Jim sometimes.
我有的时候和吉姆一起打。
Who wins?
谁赢？
I usually do.
通常是我赢。

Do you play badminton a lot, Mark?
马克，你常打羽毛球吗？
Yes, I do.
是啊，我常打。
Hey, let's play a game.
嘿，我们来场球赛吧，
You and Megan can play Jim and me.
你和梅根对吉姆和我。
Good idea! We can set up a net in the park.
好主意！我们可以在公园里架个网。
Let's play after work.
我们下班后来打。
Is that OK with you, Megan?
梅根，妳可以吗？
Sure. But plan to lose the game!
没问题。不过准备好输球吧！
Oh, no! You two will lose!
不可能！你们两个会输！
